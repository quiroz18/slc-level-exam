SLC English Placement Exam

Let's find your English level

This exam has 50 multiple-choice questions and a 1-hour time limit. You can move back and forth between questions before submitting. It can only be taken once, so make sure you're ready before you begin.

Please answer on your own, without translators, AI tools, dictionaries, or help from someone else. This exam only exists to find your real English level so your teacher can plan the right classes for you. If your result is higher than your real level because of outside help, the classes may move faster than you can actually follow — and your teacher won't be able to see your real strengths and weaknesses to help you improve. Answering honestly is what makes this useful for you.
